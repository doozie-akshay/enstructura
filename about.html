About Us

Our establishment started its operation in the year 2013 as a proprietary practice in the name of ENSTRECTURA CONSULTANTS. In the year 2017, to expand our services widely in the field of civil engineering, a private limited company called ENSTRUCTURA CONSULTANTS PRIVATE LIMITED (ECPL) was established by professionals to offer comprehensive, innovative, and state-of-the-art consultancy services.

Our expertise is developed over three decades among individuals. The core team comprises professionals with 20–30 years of experience in structural engineering, having worked with Torsteel Research Foundation in India (TRFI) and Civilaid. They were mentored by Dr. C.S. Vishwantha, one of the most renowned consultants in the field.

Our Mission

Our mission is to combine all applicable skills to resolve any critical problems in civil engineering and obtain solutions that are functionally sound, economically feasible, and practically applicable.

We strive to deliver technical excellence while upholding the highest standards of ethics and integrity in every project we undertake.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to be a trusted partner in civil and structural engineering, delivering innovative, sustainable, and reliable solutions that shape the future of infrastructure.

We strive to continuously evolve through knowledge, research, and ethical practices, making a meaningful impact in the built environment.
