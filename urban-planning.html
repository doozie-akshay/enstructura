Urban & Regional Planning & Design, Environmental Engineering

Enstructura Consultants offers comprehensive urban and regional planning services, integrating sustainable infrastructure, land use optimization, transportation systems, and livable community design. Our environmental engineering solutions focus on resource conservation, pollution control, and ecological impact mitigation, aligning projects with environmental laws and smart city principles.

What is included

Master Planning & Zoning

We develop comprehensive master plans for urban growth, including land use zoning, infrastructure alignment, transport planning, and social facility layouts to create inclusive, functional cities and regions.

Environmental Impact Assessment

We conduct detailed environmental assessments for proposed developments, addressing air, water, soil, and biodiversity concerns while preparing mitigation plans as per MOEF/SEIAA guidelines.

Water, Waste & Pollution Management

Our services include the design of water supply networks, drainage systems, STPs, ETPs, rainwater harvesting, solid waste management, and air quality monitoring plans to meet sustainability goals.

PROJECT HIGHLIGHTS

We have successfully delivered regional development plans, township layouts, and industrial cluster infrastructure with integrated environmental safeguards and long-term sustainability in mind.

  • Urban master plans and land use zoning
  • Stormwater and sewer network design
  • Environmental impact and clearance reports
  • Smart city and AMRUT-aligned proposals
